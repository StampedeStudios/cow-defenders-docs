The game is inspired by Space Invaders where the player has to eliminate all enemies without being destroyed. The player has a specific amount of health and drives a spaceship armed with a double plasma cannon.

Figure 1. Hero’s spaceship shooting enemies

Hero

Hero spaceship has two alternated plasma cannons, each clip has 45 bullets and requires 3 seconds to fully reload. The hero’s ship has 100 health points and deals more damage than basic enemy ships.

Figure 2. Hero spaceship

Enemies

There is only one type of enemy, each of them has 60 health points and deals low damage but comes in big waves.
