Enemiesüï∑Ô∏è

Enemies will walk a predefined path in waves.

Brick

They are the slaves of the alien invaders. They are faithful and sacrifice themselves for everyone else.

They are the construction material of the alien invaders.

  • Medium velocity;

  • Low Health;

  • Low Damage;

  • Typically, they come in an army;

Grob

Just above the Brick in the hierarchy of the aliens they are big and juicy.

They are instead the food of the army;

  • Slow velocity;

  • Medium health;

  • Low Damage;

  • When they die the aliens feast on their flesh and heal up;

Warrior

His favorite food is the only one they have, the Grob.

  • Medium velocity;

  • High damage;

  • Medium Health;

  • Armour;

Turrets

All the turrets are created from the scraps of the farm. The [scraps] are the currency you get when killing alien invaders, they let you build and upgrade more turrets.

Economy

Laboratory

It allows you to unlock new upgrades from the menu using Currency. The upgrades are in a tree structure. There are two kind of upgrades. The first kind is the most common one, the upgrade is available from the start of the level (more damage, faster rate of fire, cheaper costs, slow down enemy etc‚Ä¶). The second kind of upgrade can be obtained only after you have unlocked an entire branch of the tree upgrades. This upgrade is not available from the start of the level, you have to power up a turret to the max before being able to use it. This adds a unique and strong feature to a turret that can be further upgraded. Potential name of this unique upgrades is:

Power Up.

All the technologies of the farm and the laboratory are created by an AI who contacted you before the break out.

Currency

You get persistent currency at the end of the level based on your performance (damage received to your structure).

Scrap

Scrap is the fundamental resource to build and upgrade your turrets on the field.
